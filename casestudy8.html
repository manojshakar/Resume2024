Key Information

Company: Vajro

Duration : 8 weeks

Team: 1 Product Designer, 1 Product Managers, 2 Frontend Developers, 1 QA Tester

My role: Led the complete transformation of Vajro's digital experience, identifying pain points and optimizing user flows for both the admin dashboard and customer-facing platforms

Platform: Web

The Mission
Revamp Vajro's digital experience by addressing user friction and usability gaps. The aim was to enhance user engagement, conversion rates, and overall product satisfaction by improving the UX of both the admin dashboard and customer-facing platform. We sought to simplify key workflows, reduce cognitive load, and create an intuitive, scalable experience for users at every touchpoint.
